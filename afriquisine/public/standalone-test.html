Jollof Rice

Origin: West Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal)

Jollof rice is a staple dish in many West African countries. It consists of rice cooked in a rich sauce made from tomatoes, peppers, onions, and various spices. Often served with meat or fish, it's a celebratory dish enjoyed at parties and gatherings.

Bobotie

Origin: South Africa

Bobotie is a well-known South African dish consisting of spiced minced meat baked with an egg-based topping. It combines sweet and savory flavors, featuring ingredients like curry powder, dried fruit, and nuts. It's typically served with yellow rice.
