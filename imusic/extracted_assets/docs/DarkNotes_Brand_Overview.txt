DarkNotes – AI-Powered Music Mentorship Platform

Concept:
An immersive platform where up-and-coming artists receive mentorship from AI clones of legendary musicians. Features include a personalized creative journey, interactive studio, artist collaboration zones, and a digital marketplace.

Core Features:
- EchoMentors: AI-cloned mentors from top artists
- MuseLab: Creative DAW studio with lyric/beat/mentor AI assistance
- Collab Zone: Match with peers for co-creation
- HustleHub: Marketplace for beats, tools, services
- Challenge Arena: Competitions and feedback cycles

Brand:
- Name: DarkNotes
- Tagline: “Where your rawest thoughts become your realest sound.”
- Colors: Deep black, obsidian, purple haze, soft gold
- Fonts: Serif for emotional titles, clean sans-serif for UI

Visual Style:
Dark, moody, handwritten textures, glitch effects, glowing UI accents
